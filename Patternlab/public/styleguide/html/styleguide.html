Oxfam Emporium Manchester

At Oxfam Emporium we've got an extensive range of books with thousands of fiction and non-fiction titles to choose from. Unique clothing for Men and Women, ranging from every day essentials to designer pieces. Shelves and shelves of lovely homeware, perfect for gifts or to brighten up any room in your home! Vinyl, CD's, DVD's and even a few cassettes!?

The full range of Oxfam new product including; fair trade cards, food, gifts, shampoo and eco friendly cleaning products.

Whatever you're looking for you'll find it here.
